Custom Menu

The menu is used to display a menu of individual pages or external links within easy reach in the sidebar.
  • To link a wiki page, enter the full path in the Page/Link field.
  • For external links, enter the URL in the Page/Link field and provide a title in the Title field.
  • You can add FontAwesome icons by entering HTML like <i class="fas fa-question-circle"></i> in the Icon field.
  • To create a separator, set the Page/Link field to --- and leave other fields empty.
